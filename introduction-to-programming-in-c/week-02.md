**Question 1.** **Count Distinct Elements in a Sorted Sequence**\
You are given a non-decreasing sorted sequence of non-negative integers, ending with `-1`. That is, if the sequence is `a₁, a₂, … aₙ, -1`, then\
`aᵢ ≤ aᵢ₊₁` for all `i` from 1 to n-1.\
You can assume that there are at least two numbers before the ending `-1`.

You have to output the number of distinct elements in the sorted sequence (excluding the `-1`).
